Ya-Dong Wu

wuyadong301@sjtu.edu.cn

I am a tenure-track associate professor at John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research focuses on quantum information lying at the intersection of quantum physics and computer science. I am particularly interested in (1) applying machine learning for quantum information science, and (2) continuous-variable quantum information theory.
Now I have open positions for both postdoc and PhD. Please contact me if you are interested!
