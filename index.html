Ya-Dong Wu 吴亚东

yadongwu@hku.hk

I am a tenure-track associate professor at the John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research focuses on quantum information and machine learning for quantum. Before joining SJTU, I was a postdoctoral fellow in Prof. Giulio Chiribella 's group at the University of Hong Kong. I earned my Ph.D. from the University of Calgary under the supervision of Prof. Barry Sanders.

  • Bachelor, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 2008-2012
