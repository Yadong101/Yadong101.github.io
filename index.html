Ya-Dong Wu 吴亚东

yadongwu@hku.hk

I am a tenure-track associate professor at the John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research focuses on quantum information and machine learning for quantum science. Before joining SJTU, I was a postdoctoral fellow in Prof. Giulio Chiribella's group at the University of Hong Kong. I earned my Ph.D. in physics from the University of Calgary under the supervision of Prof. Barry C. Sanders.
